Dr Thomas C Reilly.

Consultation Liaison Psychiatrist. Melbourne, Australia.

About

Tom is a RANZCP accredited Consultation Liaison Psychiatrist at RMH Neuropsychiatry and Epworth Neuropsychiatry. He is a PhD Candidate through the University of Melbourne.

He has expertise in neurodegenerative conditions, neurostimulation, neuroimaging, and general consultation liaison psychiatry.
